Flow per vessel = total flow ÷ trains in service (duty only, or all units incl. standby). HLR = flow per vessel ÷ bed area. "Total" columns are system/installed totals (media and vessels across all installed trains); filter area is per train since the lag vessel shares the cross-section. EBCT has no system total, so it's shown per vessel and per train. Freeboard = straight side height − expanded bed depth.
HLR = Q/A · EBCT = BV gal / Q · bed depth = media ÷ density ÷ area · expanded = depth × (1 + expansion)
