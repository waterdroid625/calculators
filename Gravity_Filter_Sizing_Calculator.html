Grid is R rows × C columns with common walls; cell count rounds to a multiple of the selected row count. Walls: 3 longitudinal (2 exterior + 1 center, full plan length) + (C+1) transverse (spanning 2L), all at the common wall thickness and full box depth. Elevated slab area = sum of clear cell areas. EBCT/filter = bed volume (gal) ÷ flow per cell (gpm); HLR = flow per cell ÷ cell area. Badges judged at the design condition (duty cells carrying full flow, standby out for backwash). Freeboard = box depth − elevated slab − expanded media depth. Peak backwash flow assumes one cell washed at a time at the peak loading rate. SS underdrain laterals (AWI Phoenix / Ovivo folded-plate type) are laid edge-to-edge; default coverage 1.0 LF/sf reflects ~12-in-wide laterals. The $/LF default is planning-level — confirm with vendor quotes.
EBCT = BV gal ÷ cell gpm · HLR = cell gpm ÷ cell area · density lb/cf = g/cc × 62.428 · concrete priced per installed cf
