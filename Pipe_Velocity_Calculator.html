All velocities assume the pipe is flowing full. ID = OD − 2(wall) − 2(lining). Velocity head = V²/2g. Headloss is Hazen-Williams: S = [V / (1.318·C·R^0.63)]^(1/0.54), reported per 100 ft, R = ID/4 (full pipe).
Standards: Ductile iron — ANSI/AWWA C151/A21.51 (pipe), C150/A21.50 (thickness design), C104/A21.4 (cement-mortar lining). PVC — ASTM D1785 (Sch 40/80, IPS OD). HDPE — AWWA C906 / ASTM F714, sized by DR (wall = OD/DR); OD basis selectable IPS or DIPS (ductile-iron OD). Default C: cement-lined iron & PVC/HDPE 140–150, unlined iron 100; override as needed.
1 MGD = 1.5472 cfs = 694.4 gpm · ID values back-checked against published AWWA C151 cement-lined dimensions
