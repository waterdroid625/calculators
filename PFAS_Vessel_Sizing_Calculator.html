EBCT criterion is set per vessel (default 10 min), so lead/lag trains need 20 min total and single-stage need 10 min, automatically. Badges are judged at the design condition (duty trains carrying full flow), the binding case. HLR uses one vessel's cross-sectional area (series flow). Auto-size picks the smallest N that satisfies both the HLR ceiling and the per-vessel EBCT floor. Freeboard = straight wall depth minus expanded bed depth, with the bed seated on the vessel floor; it turns red if the expanded bed exceeds the straight wall.
HLR = train gpm ÷ bed area · EBCT/vessel = BV gal ÷ train gpm · density lb/cf = g/cc × 62.428 · bed depth = media ÷ density ÷ area
